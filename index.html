THE LEGION OF DOMINANCE

Peace & Harmony · Absolute Power · Eternal Legacy

September 28, 2025 — The Night It Started

Every empire has a moment of ignition. For Night Raiders, that moment came on the night of September 28th, 2025 — when Lord Devine and Lord Zephyr executed their first group hijack together.

It wasn't just a raid. It was a statement. They infiltrated a WhatsApp group, systematically bugged the admins and members, and made one thing crystal clear: remove us, and we come to your DM. The fear was real. The admins complied. Admin access was granted — and the moment it was, every admin was removed and the group was renamed For Sale.

By the end of that same night, the group had a buyer. Two men. One operation. One sale. And a feeling neither of them had felt before — the rush of building something from nothing and profiting from the chaos.

September 30, 2025 — The Empire Takes Shape

Lord Devine quickly understood one truth: hunting alone is slow. Gathering group links, scouting targets, running operations — it required more hands. On September 30th, just two days after the first hijack, he reached out to a trusted friend — Lord Obito Uchiha — and asked him to create a group.

The group existed before the name did. Lord Devine sat with it for a full day, searching for something that meant something. Then it came to him — they raided in the night. They moved while others slept. They struck when the world was quiet and unprepared. There was only one name for what they were.

Night Raiders.

The Growth

Word spread. Lord Reaper joined, bringing with him a spy network that sees everything before it happens. Lady Deedee arrived — underestimated by everyone, feared by those who knew better. Lord Anonymous, Lord Guardian, Lord Slur, Lord Deluxe and others followed — each one hand-picked, each one carrying the standard.

Night Raiders reached its peak in hijacking operations. Groups were raided, taken over, and sold. The empire was not just growing — it was earning. Fun and profit, built on precision and discipline.

On War — A Misunderstood Doctrine

Let this be clearly understood: Night Raiders was not built for direct war. Our origin is hijacking — calculated, profitable, and executed with surgical precision. We do not declare open war under the Night Raiders name. That is not weakness. That is strategy.

When our allies — clans like the Saints — need support in battle, Night Raiders answers the call. We fight. We show up. But we do so on our terms, as a force that chooses when and how it engages. Any clan that has mistaken our restraint for fear has never seen what happens when we decide to move.

The crash bots are ready. The ban bots are armed. The spy network is active. Night Raiders has never needed to go to war — because no clan has been bold enough to force our hand. That day has not yet come. But the empire is prepared.

Today

What began as two men and a single hijacked group on a quiet September night has grown into a structured empire with ranked members, defined roles, an intelligence network, and a reputation that precedes every operation.

Night Raiders is not a clan you stumble into. It is a clan you earn your place in. The badge 【☠︎】 worn by every raider and 【𖦠】 carried by the ranked few — these are not decorations. They are proof of what you survived to become.

The story is still being written. The raids are still happening. And the night is still ours.
